# WASP-69b Schlawin et al. (2024) data

These files are the machine-readable tables associated with Schlawin et al.
(2024), *Multiple Clues for Dayside Aerosols and Temperature Gradients in
WASP-69 b from a Panchromatic JWST Emission Spectrum*, AJ 168, 104.

- Article DOI: `10.3847/1538-3881/ad58e0`
- VizieR catalog: `J/AJ/168/104`
- MAST observations DOI: `10.17909/v2v9-k243`
- Download source: `https://cdsarc.cds.unistra.fr/ftp/J/AJ/168/104/`
- Downloaded: 2026-07-11

Files:

- `sp.dat`: native 280-point combined NIRCam and MIRI eclipse-depth spectrum.
- `spbin.dat`: 79-point rebinned visualization spectrum and brightness temperatures.
- `ReadMe`: CDS schema and provenance.
